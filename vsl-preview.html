VSL Preview Script - The STRONG Operator Cohort

Built: 2026-05-16 Format: Short preview VSL, 90 seconds. NOT a full 8-minute sales VSL. The hero video on the landing page is Adam's existing "10 Second Pitch | Fastest D2D Pitch For Roofing Sales" YouTube short (-U2Khv2L7x8, verified embeddable via oEmbed on The STRONG Roofer channel) until a custom 5-7 minute cohort VSL is filmed on Day 1-3 of the install. Purpose: The 90-second preview is the version Adam can record on his phone in one take and post directly to IG / TikTok / YouTube Shorts to drive applications. The landing page hero video is the longer 10-Second-Pitch placeholder plus the future custom cohort VSL.

The 90-second preview script

Total runtime: 85-95 seconds. Shoot vertical. Phone or DSLR. Single take preferred.

Beat 1 - The hook (0:00 - 0:08)

On screen (Adam direct to camera):

"13 years in roofing sales. Started in D2D, built into the COO chair at a multi-state restoration company, now running the Roof Strategist and the Roofing STRONG Alliance. Here is what nobody productises in this industry, and what I am about to."

Production note: Hook lands the credibility (the 13-year track record + the operator-to-COO arc + the founder credential) plus the curiosity (the thing nobody productises). Eight seconds total. Direct to camera, no cutaway.

Beat 2 - The diagnosis (0:08 - 0:30)

On screen (Adam, mix of direct to camera plus 1-2 cutaway shots of him at desk reviewing a branch P&L sheet or a recruiting board):

"The reason most reps get stuck at sub-$200K commission and most roofing companies get stuck at sub-$5M is almost never one thing. It is one of four. D2D and storm closing systems, recruiting and retention, branch P and L and ops, marketing and content compounding.

Every operation I have ever walked, the bottleneck is one of those four. The other three are running fine."

Production note: Twenty-two seconds. Cutaways are the b-roll bank from the photoshoot. Inside: him at a desk with a P&L sheet or a recruiting board on screen, no rep names visible.

Beat 3 - The fix (0:30 - 0:55)

On screen (Adam direct to camera, kinetic):

"The STRONG Operator Cohort is eight weeks, four modules, one per fortnight. 25 seats per cycle, application-only. Live group call every week, office hours twice a month, your operation walked on camera in week 2.

Same playbook I have run for 13 years. Same playbook the Roof Strategist course catalogue previews. The cohort is the install version of the courses."

Production note: Twenty-five seconds. This is the offer-clarity beat. Kinetic, not lecture. Cutaways: hand on a recruiting funnel doc, a screen showing the four-module map, a brief shot of a job site.

Beat 4 - The CTA (0:55 - 1:25)

On screen (Adam direct to camera, calm):

"Application is a four-minute form. Submit, pick a cycle, you are enrolled. No setter call, no back and forth.

If you have run the Sales System on doors and run the numbers on your own operation, you are halfway in. If you have not, run the 90-second Roofing Operator Score first. Link in bio.

The STRONG Operator Cohort. The courses are the pre-sell. The cohort is the install. Link in bio."

Production note: Thirty seconds. The CTA is calm, not pushy. The brand signature line "The courses are the pre-sell. The cohort is the install." anchors. Cutaways: brief shot of the cohort landing page on a laptop, brief shot of the four-module map.

Production notes

Equipment: - Phone (iPhone 14 or newer recommended) shooting 4K vertical, or a DSLR if Adam has one available - One key light or a window-light setup, no flat overhead - Mic: lavalier on the shirt collar, or a shotgun mic on a boom out of frame - Tripod or phone stabiliser

Wardrobe: - Clean brand-aligned polo or t-shirt - Work pants or jeans if the cutaway includes a job-site shot - No suit, no influencer-aesthetic loungewear

Style: - Natural light where possible - Direct, operator-grounded delivery - No "hustle" pacing - this is calm and high-conviction, matching the cadence of the 10-Second-Pitch video and the Roof Strategist Podcast

Cutaway bank for the full 5-7 minute cohort VSL (films Day 1-3 of install):

The full cohort VSL adds depth on each module. Cutaways come from the photoshoot brief (see content-strategy.md). The four-module structure is:

  1. Module 1 (Weeks 1-2) - D2D and storm-damage closing systems - Cutaways: job site (in-progress install), homeowner conversation b-roll, whiteboard with pitch-flow diagram, screen showing storm-claim documentation
  2. Module 2 (Weeks 3-4) - Recruiting and retention - Cutaways: recruiting board with anonymised tiles, interview-format setup, at-desk reviewing rep performance data
  3. Module 3 (Weeks 5-6) - Branch P and L and ops - Cutaways: P&L sheet on screen, ops dashboard b-roll, whiteboard with branch metric grid
  4. Module 4 (Weeks 7-8) - Personal-brand and content compounding - Cutaways: studio setup (the YouTube channel set), podcast mic, hand on the Roofing Sales Survival Guide book cover, screen showing The STRONG Roofer YouTube analytics

B-roll bank target: 6-8 cutaways per module. Total: 25-30 cutaways from the half-day shoot. Cutaways double as content for Lane 1 module drops (see content-strategy.md).

Full 5-7 minute custom cohort VSL - structure

Beat 1 (0:00 - 0:30) - The hook Adam direct to camera. 13-year operator credential + the curiosity ("here is what nobody productises").

Beat 2 (0:30 - 1:30) - The diagnosis The four-module map, walked at depth. One module = one bottleneck. Every operation is stuck in one of four places.

Beat 3 (1:30 - 3:00) - The cohort structure 8 weeks, 25 seats, application-only. Weekly live group call. Biweekly office hours. Private community channel. The four modules walked in order. The applicant's own operation goes on the table.

Beat 4 (3:00 - 4:00) - The proof 13 years operating, former Roofing Company COO at a multi-state $8MM/yr restoration company, Roof Strategist Sales System in 50 states + Canada + Australia + Sweden, Owens Corning Official Sales Training Partner, Roofing STRONG Alliance founder, Amazon book, multi-tens-of-thousands YouTube channel, weekly podcast.

Beat 5 (4:00 - 5:30) - The cohort cycle calendar + tier pricing hint 4 cycles per year. 25 seats per cycle. Standard tier and standard-plus-operating-review tier. Mid-tier mentorship pricing range, exact tier-pricing visible inside the four-minute application.

Beat 6 (5:30 - 6:30) - The application Four-minute Typeform. Submit, get routed to the cohort calendar, pick a cycle, enrol. No setter call. No back-and-forth. The application is a fit-check, not a gatekeeper.

Beat 7 (6:30 - 7:00) - The brand signature line + CTA "The Roof Strategist courses are the pre-sell. The STRONG Operator Cohort is the install. Apply at the link below."

Total runtime: 6-7 minutes. Filmed on Day 1-3 of install. Embedded on the landing page hero. Reuploaded to YouTube unlisted for embed performance.

VSL quality checklist (run before publish)

  • [ ] No em dashes in the script or on-screen text
  • [ ] No marketing-influencer language ("hack," "secret," "trick," "tap into," "unlock," "leverage")
  • [ ] No income or roofing-license outcome guarantees
  • [ ] The four-module structure is named explicitly
  • [ ] The cohort is positioned as the install version of the courses, not as a replacement
  • [ ] The alliance is referenced once as observable fact, not pitched
  • [ ] The TAMKO acquisition is not the headline (it is the wedge timing, mentioned briefly if at all)
  • [ ] The CTA is the four-minute application, not a setter call
  • [ ] The brand signature line "The courses are the pre-sell. The cohort is the install." lands on the final beat
  • [ ] The 10-Second-Pitch flagship video (-U2Khv2L7x8) is the placeholder hero until the custom VSL is live
