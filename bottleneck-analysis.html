Bottleneck Analysis - Adam Bensman (@roofstrategist)

Built: 2026-05-16 Status: Cold first-touch. Three things I would build differently before the post-TAMKO transition window closes. The single sharpest wedge is the cohort tier above the existing course catalogue.

The frame

You started in D2D roofing sales in 2011. Built into the COO role at a multi-state $8MM/yr restoration company. Founded The Roof Strategist as the sales-training brand. Built the Sales System into all 50 US states plus Canada, Australia, and Sweden. Named Official Sales Training Partner for Owens Corning. Authored The Roofing Sales Survival Guide on Amazon. Founded the Roofing & Solar Reform Alliance, which TAMKO acquired on May 13 and relaunched as the Roofing STRONG Alliance by TAMKO with you continuing to lead and an expanded TAMKO role at the same time. The YouTube channel renamed to The STRONG Roofer. The podcast keeps weekly cadence across Apple, Spotify, Audible, and Amazon. ~19K on Instagram. TikTok runs a working SMS demo to 303-222-7133 that drives to theroofstrategist.com/roof-sales. The Marketing Battle Pack ships at $347 with a 30-day guarantee. Complete Sales Strategy and Build Your Dream Team layer up from there.

The operating businesses are healthy. The audience is hot. The course storefront is fully operational. The brand asset (Owens Corning partner + TAMKO acquisition + 13-year operator record + Amazon book + all-50-states-plus-international reach) is the moat in roofing sales education.

What is not running is the productised mentorship cohort tier between the courses and the alliance application. The elite reps earning $250K-$500K in commission and the $1MM-$13MM roofing company owners who follow you for the team-build and operations content have no productised mid-tier offer with you on the call to enrol into. The Build Your Dream Team training is the course version of that promise; the cohort version (8 weeks, 25-seat cap, live group call with you, application-only, $3K-$8K range) is the install version that does not exist publicly today.

You have everything a $300K-$800K Year-1 cohort line needs. The architecture is the gap, and the post-acquisition window is the right moment to install it.

Three things I would build differently. All install in less than 7 days from kickoff. Money in the bank inside the first cohort cycle.

1. The wedge is the productised mentorship cohort tier above the courses

Highest revenue lever.

The Marketing Battle Pack converts at $347 with a 30-day money-back guarantee. The Complete Sales Strategy layers checklists and a personalised sales plan on top. Build Your Dream Team is the owner-tier course product for growing, paying, and scaling sales teams. The course catalogue ships. Buyers finish the courses. The question is what they enrol into next.

Today the answer is "1-1 consulting by inquiry" (for the $5MM-$13MM operator) or "more YouTube and podcast" (for everyone else). There is no live group call with you on the call, weekly, 8 weeks, biweekly office hours, and the cohort applicant's own operation on the table as a live working asset. That is the upgrade tier the Build Your Dream Team buyers and the elite reps signal for, and that is not currently enrollable.

The fix is one cohort.

The STRONG Operator Cohort (working name, leans on the post-rename brand asset - The STRONG Roofer channel and the Roofing STRONG Alliance share the same naming convention) at $3K-$8K per seat, 25-seat cap per cycle, 8 weeks per cohort, 4 cycles per year. Application-only. Live weekly group call with you. Biweekly office hours. Private community channel for the 8 weeks. Four modules walked in order on the live call: D2D and storm-damage closing systems (the Sales System operationalised at the cohort level), recruiting and retention for the sales team (the Build Your Dream Team material walked live), branch P&L and ops for the $1MM-$13MM operator, personal-brand and content compounding for the sales-leader.

The hero block on the sales page anchors against the flagship YouTube video "10 Second Pitch | Fastest D2D Pitch For Roofing Sales" (verified embeddable via oEmbed, The STRONG Roofer channel) plus the trust strip: 13+ years operating, Roof Strategist Sales System in all 50 states + Canada + Australia + Sweden, Owens Corning Official Sales Training Partner, founder of the Roofing STRONG Alliance by TAMKO, author of The Roofing Sales Survival Guide. That stack is the rarest credibility wall on any roofing sales training prospect.

Math: 4 cohort cycles per year x 25 seats x $3K-$8K average = $300K-$800K direct Year-1, with cohort grads routing into the Roofing STRONG Alliance application as the natural high-touch back-end (TAMKO-owned, but you-led) and back to the course catalogue as the perpetual feeder of next-cycle applicants.

Time cost on you: weekly group call (60-90 min) + biweekly office hours block (90 min) DURING 8-week cycles = ~4-6 hours per week during cycles. ~160 hours per year across 4 cycles. Lower than the podcast-plus-blog-plus-YouTube cadence you currently maintain on top of the alliance work.

Cost of waiting: ~$75K-$200K per cohort cycle never collected. Each quarter the cohort does not exist is one cycle missed and one group of Build Your Dream Team buyers who finished the course and exited the funnel because there was no upgrade tier to enrol into. The post-TAMKO transition window is the right moment because attention is on the brand handoff, the audience is paying attention, and the cohort positions cleanly as Adam's owned mentorship layer that feeds the alliance.

Six months from now: ~$150K-$400K in cohort revenue never collected, and the Build Your Dream Team buyers continue to graduate without a paid upgrade tier.

Fix: The STRONG Operator Cohort, standard tier $3K / standard-plus-operating-review tier $8K with a 1-1 ops audit and three follow-up 1-1 calls in the 90 days post-cohort. Year-1 floor $300K-$800K direct plus incremental alliance-application volume plus course-catalogue uplift.

2. No segmenting quiz lead magnet on the front end

Fast install.

The FREE Roof Claims Crash Course is a 30+ video library that captures email at signup. That works as a free-content gate. What it does not do is segment the audience by buyer type (rep / elite-rep / owner / storm-specialist), revenue band ($0-$500K / $500K-$2M / $2M-$5M / $5M-$13M / $13M+), or primary bottleneck (closing / recruiting / ops / marketing).

The result: every email subscriber gets the same nurture sequence regardless of whether they are a brand-new rep buying the Marketing Battle Pack or a $4M operator ready to enrol in the cohort.

A "Roofing Operator Score" 12-question scorecard solves this in one move.

The flow: a viewer enters current role, current monthly revenue, current team size, current closing ratio, current recruiting cadence, current retention rate on new hires, primary lead source, current marketing spend, primary bottleneck self-rated, current training stack, AOV on jobs, and storm vs retail mix. The scorecard returns a 0-100 score, a 3-line bottleneck readout (named at the primary lever: closing systems, team build, ops, or marketing), an upgrade-path scored against the Marketing Battle Pack -> Complete Sales Strategy -> Build Your Dream Team -> Cohort ladder, and a three-line action list. Email captures at the bottom in exchange for the saved scorecard.

Segmentation tags by role (rep / elite-rep / owner / storm-specialist), revenue band, and primary bottleneck. Routes: - Rep + sub-$200K personal revenue + closing-bottleneck -> Marketing Battle Pack ($347) - Elite rep + $250K-$500K personal commission + recruiting or ops-bottleneck -> Complete Sales Strategy + scorecard-driven cohort pre-sell - Owner + $1MM-$5MM company revenue + ops or recruiting-bottleneck -> Build Your Dream Team + cohort pre-sell - Owner + $5MM-$13MM company revenue + any bottleneck -> direct cohort application + 1-1 consulting routing

Posted to the podcast end-roll on a regular cadence (the highest-intent audience), routed into the @roofstrategist IG bio-link router as the primary CTA, dropped into the TikTok caption stack alongside the existing SMS demo trigger, embedded in the YouTube descriptions, and pinned to the LinkedIn header.

Math: 5-8% audience-to-quiz floor on a multi-tens-of-thousands warm audience across YouTube + podcast + IG + TikTok = 3K-6K incremental scorecard completions in 90 days, 8K-15K in 6 months. 60-75% of scorecard completers convert to email opt-in at the moment of result delivery = 2K-4.5K incremental email subscribers from existing audience inside 90 days, 5K-11K inside 6 months.

Year-1 floor on the email layer alone: $60K-$180K in nurture-driven course catalogue purchases + cohort applications + 1-1 consulting inquiries. Compounds for life of the list.

Cost of waiting: ~$5K-$15K/mo in nurture-driven course + cohort revenue not happening. ~$60K-$180K/yr compounding for life of the list. Every cohort cycle that opens without the email asset costs more to fill via raw audience reach.

Six months from now: ~$30K-$90K never captured, and the email list is six months smaller than it should be when the cohort opens.

Fix: Roofing Operator Score on ScoreApp or Typeform. Podcast end-roll plus IG bio-link router plus TikTok caption stack plus YouTube description plus LinkedIn header. 14-day welcome flow segmented by role + revenue band + primary bottleneck. $60K-$180K Year-1 lift, compounds for life of the list.

3. The post-acquisition cross-sell between theroofstrategist.com and strongbytamko.com is unbuilt

Compound pickup. This is the rare cross-sell lever and the window is open for 90 days, not 12 months.

On May 13, TAMKO acquired RSRA's assets and relaunched as the Roofing STRONG Alliance by TAMKO. You continue leading the alliance with an expanded TAMKO role at the same time. The alliance now lives at strongbytamko.com. The course storefront lives at theroofstrategist.com. The YouTube channel renamed to The STRONG Roofer. The IG, TikTok, and short-form handles stay at @roofstrategist.

Today the two surfaces operate as separate brand entities. The alliance founding story mentions you. The course storefront mentions the alliance. The cross-sell architecture - the routing path that takes a cohort grad into the alliance application, or that takes an alliance member into the course catalogue + cohort feeder - is not visibly productised on either surface.

That gap costs cross-sell economics every day, and the window is fresh post-acquisition.

A Build Your Dream Team buyer who runs a $3M roofing company is a Roofing STRONG Alliance application candidate. Today there is no clean routing path from the course product back to the alliance application because the brand handoff is informal on the storefront side.

A Roofing STRONG Alliance member who joins for the TAMKO Edge contractor program is a Cohort candidate plus a Complete Sales Strategy buyer. Today there is no clean routing path from the alliance back to your owned content + course catalogue.

Unifying the visual + verbal brand handoff across both surfaces opens both cross-sell paths simultaneously. The work is brand-architecture, not new content production.

The work: Build the cross-sell architecture across both surfaces. On theroofstrategist.com, add a clear "Members of the Roofing STRONG Alliance get [X] inside the cohort + course catalogue" mechanic. On strongbytamko.com, work with the TAMKO team to add a clear "Roofing STRONG Alliance members who want to deepen their sales operations get [X] from Adam Bensman's owned cohort + courses" handoff (TAMKO-owned page, but a structured collaboration during the integration window). Update the @roofstrategist + The STRONG Roofer + LinkedIn bio links to route audience to the right destination by audience segment. Tier-clarify the funnel on each surface so the Build Your Dream Team buyer sees the cohort upgrade tier + the alliance application as the natural next steps. Drop an alliance handoff CTA into the post-cohort survey for qualifying cohort grads.

The math on the cross-sell lift: the cross-sell economics in a multi-brand business of this scale typically run 5-15% incremental revenue from clean tier-clarity post-acquisition. Applied to the combined course catalogue + cohort + alliance-application volume, the cross-sell variant adds another $80K-$250K Year-1.

Cost of waiting: ~$7K-$20K/mo in cross-sell economics not happening between the storefront and the alliance. ~$80K-$250K/yr compounded across the post-acquisition window.

Six months from now: another six months of Build Your Dream Team buyers exiting without an alliance handoff, and another six months of alliance members exiting without a cohort or course-catalogue handoff. The post-acquisition window is the right moment to install this because the brand integration is fresh and the audience is paying attention; in 12-18 months the integration will be settled and the cross-sell will feel retrofit rather than aligned.

Fix: Cross-sell architecture across both surfaces. Bio-link router updates on every handle. Tier-clarified funnel on each surface. Cross-sell CTAs planted into the post-cohort survey + the post-purchase course flows. $80K-$250K Year-1 attributable lift from cross-sell economics + attribution-tracking clarity.
